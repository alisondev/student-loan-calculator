Introduction

Managing student loans is never easy, but it doesn't have to involve guesswork or squinting at spreadsheets.

This is a simple calculator you can use to plan your payments. Use it to experiment with how increasing or decreasing the monthly payment, changing the interest rate (through refinancing), or reducing the current balance (by making a one-time payment) will impact the repayment term and the total cost of the loan.

Instructions

In the form below, input your current loan balance, interest rate, and intended monthly payment. The calculator will determine how many payments it will take to reach a zero balance, the total amount you'll pay, including interest, and the estimated payoff date.
